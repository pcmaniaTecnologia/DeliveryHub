# **App Name**: DeliveryHub

## Core Features:

- SaaS Structure: Multi-tenant system allowing multiple businesses to register and manage their operations independently with super admin panel to manage companies and plans.
- Dashboard Analytics: Dashboard displaying sales charts, product performance, and order statistics with customizable date filters.
- Real-Time Order Management: Display of orders in real-time with status updates, sound notifications, and automated WhatsApp status updates using customizable templates.
- Delivery/Shipping Module: Configuration of delivery zones, fees per neighborhood, fixed shipping costs, and free shipping thresholds.
- Business Configuration: Customization options for each business, including logo, contact information, business hours, theme colors, and payment methods.
- Product Catalog Management: Management of product listings with details, variants, images, and quick actions for editing and duplication.
- Coupon System: Creation and management of discount coupons with usage limits, validity dates, and automatic validation at checkout.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to evoke trust and efficiency.
- Background color: Light blue (#E0F7FA), providing a clean and calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Analogous cyan color (#29E2D1) for interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for a clean and modern look.
- Use modern, flat icons related to delivery, food, and commerce.
- Clean and intuitive dashboard layout with clear data visualization for quick insights.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience during order updates and navigation.
